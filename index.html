About Us

Mridu Beauty Parlour

Mridu Beauty Parlour is a trusted beauty destination located in the heart of Kumarikata, Assam, serving clients from Rangia, Tamulpur, and the surrounding areas. We are dedicated to helping every woman look and feel her absolute best through a wide range of professional beauty services delivered with care, skill, and the latest techniques.

Our parlour offers a warm, welcoming environment where you can relax and rejuvenate. From everyday grooming to bridal makeovers, our experienced beauticians are committed to bringing out your natural beauty. We are open six days a week from 9:00 AM and on Sundays from 10:00 AM, making it easy for you to book an appointment at your convenience.

  • Open 7 days a week including Sundays for your convenience
  • Experienced and skilled beauticians with expertise in bridal and everyday looks
  • Hygienic, welcoming salon environment in the heart of Kumarikata, Assam
